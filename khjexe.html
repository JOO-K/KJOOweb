KHJ.EXE

KHJ.EXE website link

This website was the final project for Motion, Sound, Vision at RISD. It is a digital time capsule that preserves a sense of my existence on the day of Nov. 4th, 2020 (The day I started working on the project). On the website are nine compartments containing an abstracted form of different parts of my body. Each compartment attempts to express my thoughts on the relevant body part through various forms of media.
