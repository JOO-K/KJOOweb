How can designers visualize the emotion and personalization of the arts into the design of a website?

https://joo-k.github.io/KHJDOTEXE/

This website was the final project for Motion, Sound, Vision at RISD. It is a digital time capsule that is inspired by On Kawara's Date Paintings. it preserves a sense of my existence on the day of Nov. 4th, 2020 (The day I started working on the project). The website features a 3D rendering environment by implementing Three.js, as well as assets sourced through Google Earth that were captured through RenderDoc, an open source MIT program. Through these programs, I managed to construct a recreation of my physical surroundings into digital media. The website explores the usage of web interfaces under non-commercial intentions.
