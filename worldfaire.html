19xx World Faire

19xx app download link

19xx World Faire is an assignment for Motion, Sound, Vision at RISD. It involves translating and displaying an array of 12 items. I decided to go with media that is no longer copyright infringible from the mid 1900's. This 3D digital gallery provides a glimpse into a distant world not too different from our own. Many of the thoughts and sentiments of the past resonate throughout this corridor of frozen time. The application for the gallery was developed through Unity and the rooms were assembled through open source media.
