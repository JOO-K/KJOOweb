How can online galleries facilitate theses formats across multiple forms of media?

http://vcs-21.nyc/

Working alongside the Visual Critical Studies department of SVA in New York, I had the opportunity to make exhibition graphics and promotion material along with a responsive website showcasing student theses for the senior class of 2021. The show, "3Dream Spiral_Relax," went live in January of 2021. The challenge was to create a modular display that accomodates the various media seen in the senior theses in a web format.
