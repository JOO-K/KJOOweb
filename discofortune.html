Disco Fortune Online Game Link

Disco Fortune is a 2.5D video game developed as a final prototype for Game Design at RISD. The art and graphics are inspired by Ape Inc's classic Earthbound. Go and bump to some beats at the Disco Fortune in town! The game is scripted in C# using the Unity engine.
