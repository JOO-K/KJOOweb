"—(Crack Couture) has evolved into a creative jewelry brand inspired by themes of childhood nostalgia and the growing pains of transitioning into adult age."
-CC

crackcouture.com

Working alongside Crack Couture's design team for their 2021 new season merchandise, I helped in the launching of their catalogue by creating graphics for their social media marketing and product packaging. The goal was to make printed matter that corresponded with the design theme of Crack Couture to be inlcuded alongside the purchase of their fall 2020 merchandise. The mascot, the Bunny, represents the thematic inspiration of images and iconography from adolescence that permeate this catalogue.

Mesh and Renders done in Blender.
