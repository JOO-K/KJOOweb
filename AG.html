arlettegeller.com

In collaboration with Arlette Geller, a biomedical engineer at Duke University, I helped design and launch a website displaying her various designs for medical instruments. Arlette makes use of CAD software such as 3DS Max and AutoCAD to prototype and 3D-print her work. The website needed to accommodate and display these 3D objects in a more accessible manner, as well as display final renders for product use. With the use of Blender, an open source 3D Computer Graphics program, I created high quality renders of Arlette's designs to be featured throughout the website.
